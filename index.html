Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

Introduction

Appiversal ("we", "our", or "us") operates the NStop App mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect user information when using the app.

About the App

NStop App is designed for authorized NStop users to submit and manage monthly achievement reporting data through a secure mobile platform.

Information We Collect

Authenticated user accounts may include:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Province
  • District

The app does not collect:

  • Device location
  • Camera data
  • Photos or media files
  • Contacts
  • Tracking identifiers

How We Use Information

Information is used only for:

  • User authentication
  • Displaying user profile information
  • Submitting monthly achievement reports
  • Synchronizing offline reports
  • Maintaining app functionality

Offline Storage

NStop App supports offline functionality. Data entered by users may be temporarily stored locally on the device until synchronization is completed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Internet Access

The app requires internet access for authentication and data synchronization.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect user information and locally stored data.

User Accounts and Authentication

Access to the app is restricted to authorized users through a login system.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.
